# Product Guide: Des Moines AI Pulse

## Initial Concept
A modern web platform showcasing Des Moines events, restaurants, and local insights powered by AI.

## Target Users
The primary target users for the Des Moines AI Pulse platform are local residents seeking events and dining options. The platform aims to serve their needs by providing relevant and timely information to enhance their local experiences.

## Core Functionalities
The core functionality of the platform will focus on providing personalized event and restaurant recommendations. This will enable users to discover new places and activities tailored to their interests and preferences.

## Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
The unique selling proposition of Des Moines AI Pulse is its hyper-personalized content, powered by a sophisticated AI engine. This AI will analyze user preferences and local data to offer highly relevant and unique recommendations, differentiating it from generic local guides.

## User Experience (UX) and Visual Aesthetic
The platform will aim for a modern, intuitive, and visually appealing design with easy navigation. The user experience will prioritize clarity and ease of use, ensuring that users can effortlessly discover and engage with local content.
