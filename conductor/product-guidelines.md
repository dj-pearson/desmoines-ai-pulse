# Product Guidelines: Des Moines AI Pulse

## Prose Style
All user-facing content on the "Des Moines AI Pulse" platform will adopt an **informative and engaging** tone and style. The goal is to present information in a way that is not only clear and factual but also captivating and enjoyable for users to read, enhancing their connection to the content.

## Brand Messaging
The core brand message will be centered on **local discovery and connection**. The platform will emphasize the joy of finding new experiences within Des Moines and fostering a stronger connection to the local community. This message should be reflected in all marketing and in-product communications.

## Visual Identity
The platform's visual identity will be **modern and vibrant**. This aesthetic will be achieved through the use of bright colors, clean typography, and contemporary design elements that reflect the dynamic and lively spirit of Des Moines.

## User Interaction and Feedback
The platform will be designed to be **interactive and responsive**. User actions will receive immediate and clear feedback, and the system will actively encourage user contributions, reviews, and suggestions to foster a sense of community ownership and continuous improvement.
